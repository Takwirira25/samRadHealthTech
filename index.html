About Us: Empowering Health and Research in Zimbabwe

samRad Health Technologies is a visionary health technology enterprise dedicated to transforming cancer care and fostering medical research, starting with Zimbabwe. Our core mission is to pioneer intelligent, automated systems that empower clinicians to detect and quantify tumors from radiological imaging with unparalleled precision and ease. Our AI pipelines are open, transparent, and specifically designed to accelerate diagnostics and significantly enhance medical research, ultimately benefiting patients in our home country and beyond.

Founded by Samuel Khumbula (Takwirira, Barachel), a Zimbabwean PhD student in Physics at the University of New Hampshire in the United States (starts Fall 2025, God willing), this initiative is deeply rooted in a passion for oncology and a heartfelt, unwavering commitment to expanding access to quality care within Zimbabwe. Coming from a background marked by economic hardship and limited access to advanced healthcare, Samuel has firsthand experience of the profound challenges faced by many cancer patients in our communities.

Through resilience, dedication, and a relentless pursuit of education, Samuel now seeks to leverage his expertise to transform lives—not just through technological innovation, but by creating sustainable pathways for improved health outcomes and research capabilities back home in Zimbabwe. samRad Health Technologies' primary aspiration is to establish life-changing partnerships with hospitals and research institutions across Zimbabwe, uplifting cancer care through technological excellence, shared knowledge, and a commitment to local capacity building. My education in the United States is a crucial step to acquire the specialized knowledge and skills necessary to bring these advanced solutions directly to Zimbabwe, where they are most needed.

Our Services: Driving Innovation for Better Health in Zimbabwe

AI-Powered Tumor Detection

Instant analysis of medical scan images (DICOM) to assess cancer risk using advanced, expertly trained AI models. This will allow for earlier and more accurate diagnoses, particularly in resource-constrained settings like Zimbabwe.

Clinical Research Pipeline Tools

Command-line tools designed to empower clinical researchers to process imaging data and patient datasets efficiently at scale, fostering local research capabilities and contributions to global medical knowledge within Zimbabwe.

Data Integration & Interoperability

Robust support for structured CSV metadata, clinical tagging, and export-ready research formats. Our systems are built to seamlessly integrate with existing hospital systems, ensuring practical and impactful implementation in Zimbabwean healthcare settings.

Imaging and Quiz: Empowering Patients and Clinicians in Zimbabwe

Medical imaging—like MRI, CT, and PET scans—is often the first critical step in diagnosing and treating cancer. These tools guide crucial decisions and shape patient care, yet many people, especially in underserved regions like parts of Zimbabwe, don’t fully understand what they are or how they work. Our aim is to demystify this process and make information more accessible.

Early detection is especially critical for cancers like breast cancer, prostate cancer, and glioblastoma. Catching these diseases in their earliest stages can significantly improve treatment options, reduce the need for aggressive interventions, and dramatically boost survival rates. This is particularly vital in Zimbabwe, where late-stage diagnoses are common due to limited access to advanced diagnostic tools and awareness.

At samRad Health Technologies, we’re developing advanced software that helps detect tumors directly from medical images (DICOM files)—making diagnosis faster, earlier, and more accessible for patients and healthcare providers in Zimbabwe. Our ultimate goal is to support timely intervention, enable emerging treatment modalities, and ultimately improve patient outcomes across our nation.

Being informed can reduce fear, improve communication with your care team, and help you make confident choices about your health journey.

Take the quiz to learn the basics and better navigate your health journey. Our quizzes shuffle 10 questions each time from a growing pool of oncology facts, with new questions replenished weekly. Our goal is to educate and empower, and we hope to translate all this valuable information into local languages to ensure widespread understanding across Zimbabwe.
